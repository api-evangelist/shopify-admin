App quality checks for published apps are now managed through the Partner Dashboard's App Distribution section, providing the same review management experience as new app submissions. This unified experience includes requirement-level tracking, a structured workflow, and direct messaging, allowing developers to view audit feedback and manage updates in one centralized location rather than monitoring email.
